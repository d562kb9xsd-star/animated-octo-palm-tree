How the system works

Simple public reporting with moderated publication control.

1. Submit

A witness files a report with summary, location, date, and optional uploaded evidence.

2. Review

Moderators inspect the material, verify completeness, and assign a final status.

3. Release

Approved case files are published to the archive, map, and individual case pages.

Public access points

Browse cases, inspect locations, or submit your own report.

Use the archive for released files, the map for location-based sightings, and the submission portal for new reports.
